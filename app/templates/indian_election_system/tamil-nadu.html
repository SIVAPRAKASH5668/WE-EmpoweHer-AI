Political Parties

  • DMK: Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam, led by M. K. Stalin. Major regional party in Tamil Nadu.
  • AIADMK: All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam, led by O. Panneerselvam and Edappadi K. Palaniswami.
  • BJP: Bharatiya Janata Party, with increasing influence in Tamil Nadu in recent years.
  • Congress: Indian National Congress, which has had a presence in Tamil Nadu but less influence in recent years.
  • VCK: Viduthalai Chiruthaigal Katchi, a party focused on Dalit rights and social justice.
